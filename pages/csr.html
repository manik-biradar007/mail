Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) of an organisation to make an impact on society at large by its decisions and activities. Beyond Corporation India Pvt. Ltd. (BCIPL) has invested in local communities to add value and make a significant and lasting impact. It is said that ‘strong communities support strong organizations’; we believe that the reverse is also true. Through our responsible and transparent approach, we aspire to build a sustainable future for all!

BCIPL has supported local art and culture by being the prime sponsors for ‘Sangeetounmesh’ at Shanivarwada, Pune, which is a National Level Musical programme, held for three days in 2014.

Mr. Parag Mate, spear-heading the CSR at BCIPL has been a conscious citizen through his strategic business plans incorporating CSR activities in the religious, sports, education and other areas for common good of all.

Respecting Religious Sentiments

  • Monetary contribution for Shri Bhairavnath Vitthal Mandir, Khadakwasla (President).
  • Monetary contribution for the Rajyabhishek of Shivaji Maharaj and Pai Palkhi Sohala.
  • Monetary contribution to Maan Devi Temple.
  • Monetary contribution to Mahalunge Temple.

Encouraging Sports

  • Donations to Pune Kushti Aayojan Raksha Seva Yuva Manch.

Access to Education for all

  • Donations to Gyan Jyoti Shikshan and Sanskrutik Sanstha.
  • Donations to Zilla Parishad School, Khadakwasla.
  • Donations to Khadakwasla Taleem.

Youth Empowerment

  • Donations to Yug Pravartak trust every month since last 3 years.
